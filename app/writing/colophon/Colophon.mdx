# Colophon

## Typography

Built using [Inter](https://rsms.me/inter/), a free and open source font family designed to be readable, clean and has plenty of glyphs.

## Photographs

All images, videos, and other forms of media were taken by me unless otherwise explicitly stated. Duplication, reuse, or publication is not permitted without the written permission of the owner.

## Stack

This site is made using [Next.js](https://nextjs.org) and [React](https://react.dev), coded in Typescript, and deployed to [Vercel](https://vercel.com), partially refined by AI. [Tailwind](https://tailwindcss.com) is used for styling, and [Motion](https://motion.dev) used for animations.

## Design

Set in the Inter typeface, using minimalist and clean design. White text on black background with a simple and clear layout.

## Inspiration

The design of this site is inspired by [Linus Rogge](https://linusrogge.com) and many other sites from [Dead Simple Sites's](https://deadsimplesites.com) list.
